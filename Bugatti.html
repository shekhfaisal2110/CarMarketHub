Bugatti Veyron

The Bugatti Veyron is a legendary hypercar that redefined the boundaries of speed and performance. With a top speed of over 250 mph, this masterpiece combines an 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine with cutting-edge aerodynamics and technology. Designed for the ultimate driving enthusiast, the Veyron is as much a piece of art as it is a marvel of engineering, blending extreme performance with unmatched luxury.

Key Features

  • 8.0L W16 quad-turbocharged engine
  • 1,001 horsepower and 922 lb-ft of torque
  • 7-speed dual-clutch transmission and all-wheel drive
  • 0-60 mph in 2.5 seconds
  • Top speed of 253 mph (406 km/h)
  • Carbon fiber body and luxury leather interior
