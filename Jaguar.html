Jaguar F-Type

The Jaguar F-Type is a stunning sports car that blends exhilarating performance with a striking design. Available in coupe and convertible forms, the F-Type offers a thrilling driving experience, featuring a range of powerful engines, including supercharged V6 and V8 options. With its agile handling, luxurious interior, and advanced technology, the F-Type delivers both style and substance.

Key Features

  • 5.0L supercharged V8 engine
  • 575 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque
  • 8-speed automatic transmission with rear-wheel drive
  • 0-60 mph in 3.5 seconds
  • Adaptive dynamics suspension system for responsive handling
  • Luxurious leather interior with advanced infotainment system
