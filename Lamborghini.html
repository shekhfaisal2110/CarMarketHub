Lamborghini Urus

The Lamborghini Urus is a high-performance luxury SUV that blends the raw power and exhilarating speed of a supercar with the practicality and elegance of an SUV. With a twin-turbocharged V8 engine, all-wheel drive, and aggressive styling, the Urus redefines what an SUV can be. It offers an extraordinary driving experience while maintaining Lamborghini's signature bold design and uncompromising luxury.

Key Features

  • 4.0L twin-turbocharged V8 engine
  • 657 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque
  • 8-speed automatic transmission with all-wheel drive
  • 0-60 mph in 3.2 seconds
  • Top speed of 190 mph (305 km/h)
  • Luxurious interior with premium leather and advanced tech
