Rolls-Royce Ghost

The Rolls-Royce Ghost is a luxury sedan known for its refined elegance, exceptional comfort, and powerful performance. Powered by a 6.75-liter V12 engine, the Ghost combines incredible horsepower with a smooth, whisper-quiet ride. It's designed for those who demand the very best in automotive craftsmanship, technology, and luxury.

Key Features

  • 6.75L V12 twin-turbocharged engine
  • 562 horsepower, 627 lb-ft of torque
  • 8-speed automatic transmission
  • 0-60 mph in 4.6 seconds
  • Luxurious hand-stitched leather interior
  • Advanced driver assistance features
