BMW X5

The 2020 BMW X5 is a luxury midsize SUV that blends powerful performance, cutting-edge technology, and refined comfort. With an aggressive stance, a spacious and luxurious cabin, and impressive on-road dynamics, the X5 is engineered to deliver an elevated driving experience, whether you're navigating city streets or exploring off-road terrain.

Key Features

  • Engine: 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 or 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8
  • Horsepower: 335 hp (I6) / 523 hp (V8)
  • Fuel Economy: 21 MPG City / 26 MPG Highway (I6)
  • Infotainment System: iDrive 7.0 with a 12.3-inch touchscreen, Apple CarPlay, and Android Auto
  • Safety: Standard Active Driving Assistant (Lane Departure Warning, Blind Spot Detection, Forward Collision Warning)
  • All-Wheel Drive: xDrive (AWD) system for optimal traction and performance
