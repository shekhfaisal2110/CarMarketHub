Ford Mustang

The 2022 Ford Mustang continues the legendary muscle car legacy with its aggressive styling, powerful engine options, and modern tech features. Whether you're looking for an everyday sports car or a track-ready performance machine, the Mustang offers a range of configurations to suit every driver. From the classic V8 GT to the eco-friendly EcoBoost engine, the Mustang is designed for performance enthusiasts and car lovers alike.

Key Features

  • Engine Options: 2.3L EcoBoost 4-cylinder, 5.0L V8
  • Horsepower: 310 hp (EcoBoost) / 450 hp (GT V8)
  • Torque: 350 lb-ft (EcoBoost) / 410 lb-ft (GT V8)
  • Fuel Economy: 21 MPG City / 29 MPG Highway (EcoBoost)
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual or 10-speed automatic
  • Infotainment: SYNC 3 with Apple CarPlay & Android Auto, 8-inch touchscreen
