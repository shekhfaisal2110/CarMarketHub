Toyota Camry

The 2021 Toyota Camry is a mid-size sedan known for its reliability, comfort, and fuel efficiency. It is equipped with a 2.5L 4-cylinder engine that offers excellent gas mileage and a smooth ride.

Key Features

  • Fuel Efficiency: 28 MPG City / 39 MPG Highway
  • Engine: 2.5L 4-Cylinder
  • Horsepower: 203 hp
  • Transmission: 8-Speed Automatic
  • Safety: Toyota Safety Sense 2.5+
  • Infotainment: 9-Inch Touchscreen, Apple CarPlay, Android Auto
